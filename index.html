About

内容是产品，数据是证据。Content is a product. Data is the proof.

我开始做内容，是想验证一件事：好内容能不能不靠投放，自己赢得观众？三年后答案是肯定的——36,000+ 互动，一篇一篇累积出来。

比起爆红，我更在意"为什么它有效"：哪个钩子换来收藏，哪张封面换来点击。所以我复盘每一篇的数据，甚至自己写工具去分析它。我把内容当产品：研究受众、验证假设、读数据、迭代。

对品牌来说，这意味着我做的不只是"好看"的东西，而是人们会收藏、转发、并采取行动的内容。旅行、美食、生活方式是我的画布；数据是我判断它是否奏效的方式。

I started creating to test one idea: can good content earn its audience without paid promotion? Three years later the answer is yes — 36,000+ engagements, built one post at a time.

I care less about going viral and more about why something works — which hook earns a save, which cover earns a tap. So I track every post and even build my own tools to analyze it. I treat content like a product: research the audience, test a hypothesis, read the data, iterate.

For a brand, that means I don't just make things that look good — I make content people save, share and act on. Travel, food and lifestyle are my canvas; data is how I know it's working.

— Low Kee Sing (Andy) · Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Case 01 — 本地攻略 / 爆款Local Guide / Viral

吉隆坡天后宫拜月老攻略The Most Detailed Yue Lao Guide in KL

一支把"打卡话题"做成"保姆级教学"的短视频，成为我全账号表现最好的内容。A short video that turned a check-in topic into a step-by-step tutorial — my single best-performing post.

Challenge
吉隆坡有大量寺庙打卡照，却没有一份清楚的月老参拜流程，游客普遍搞不懂步骤。KL had plenty of temple check-in photos but no clear how-to for praying to Yue Lao — visitors were confused about the steps.
Strategy
抢占"全网最细"这一定位，把打卡题改成教学题——高情绪价值（姻缘）+ 强本地属性。Own the "most detailed" position; convert a check-in topic into a tutorial — high emotional value (love) plus strong local relevance.
Execution
梳理完整参拜流程脚本，实地拍摄，CapCut 剪辑竖版短视频，用"全网最细"制造好奇钩子。Scripted the full ritual, shot on-site, edited a tight vertical video in CapCut, wrote a curiosity-gap hook.

Case 02 — 开业 Campaign / 多形式Launch Campaign / Multi-format

Immersify KL 开业：两种形式抢占新地标Owning a Landmark Launch, in Two Formats

围绕大马首个永久性沉浸式媒体艺术馆开幕，用"图文 + 视频"两篇打一场 campaign。A two-post campaign around the opening of Malaysia's first permanent immersive media art gallery.

Challenge
高关注度的新场馆，开业前存在信息差；但窗口期很短，所有博主很快都会跟进。A high-interest new venue with an information gap before opening — and a short window before every creator covered it.
Strategy
抢先发布，并覆盖游客决策的两个阶段：图文负责"告知＋被收藏"，视频负责"信任＋临场感"。Move first and cover two stages of the visitor journey — a photo post to inform and be saved, a video to build trust and atmosphere.
Execution
开幕前发 17 图详解（时间、看点），开幕当天补一支实拍视频。一个选题，两种形式，两波流量。A 17-image explainer before opening (dates, what to expect), then a same-day on-site video. One topic, two formats, two traffic waves.

Case 03 — 信任内容 / 高收藏Trust Content / High Intent

把一张收据做成可分享的实用干货Turning a Receipt into Shareable Utility

一篇关于医疗开销的亲身经验帖——马来西亚人最关心的民生话题之一。A personal-experience post on healthcare cost — a topic every Malaysian cares about.

Challenge
民生／价格类内容枯燥且容易被质疑，如何让费用拆解既可信、又值得被存下来？Utility topics are dry and easy to distrust. How to make a cost breakdown people believe and keep?
Strategy
用价格反差制造钩子（RM38 vs 私院数千），再用真实收据和完整流程佐证——真实性就是护城河。Lead with a price-shock hook (RM38 vs thousands), then prove it with real receipts and the full process — authenticity as the moat.
Execution
5 图笔记，真实凭证，诚实平实的文案，主动引导评论区经验交流。A 5-image post, real documentation, plain honest copy, inviting discussion in the comments.
